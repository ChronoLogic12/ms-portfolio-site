About Me

I am an aspiring junior web developer/designer currently in the process of completing a level 5 NVQ in web app development with Code Institute. After independently studying coding though Codecademy and finding it engaging and fulfilling I decided in 2021 to pursue web development as a career. I have since completed several projects both front end and full stack which can be viewed in my portfolio below. With almost 8 years of experience in specialised, customer facing roles in retail environments I am highly capable with regards to communication and working as part of a team. In addition to my understanding of programming languages and frameworks I am proficient with Adobe Photoshop and XD and have aided other developers with overall website design and graphics for their projects.
